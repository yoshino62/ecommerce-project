Who We Are?

Burns Antique Store began as a family passion, lovingly passed down from father to daughter. Founded by a man with an eye for quality and a talent for restoring beauty, the store grew from his deep knowledge of antiques and his dedication to breathing new life into timeless furniture. Antiquing was more than a hobby; it was the bond that brought father and daughter together, with countless hours spent discovering and restoring unique treasures. Today, his daughter proudly carries on his legacy, bringing that same passion and expertise to every piece in the collection at Burns Antique Store. Here, we honor the past, cherish our memories, and offer you the chance to bring a bit of history into your own home.
